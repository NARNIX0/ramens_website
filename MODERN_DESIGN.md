```css

--color-dark: #0F172A     (Deep slate - main dark)
--color-light: #F8FAFC    (Ultra-light gray - backgrounds)

```

### Color Philosophy
- **Vibrant accent** (coral orange) for CTAs and highlights
- **Clean backgrounds** (near-white) for maximum readability
- **Modern slate tones** instead of harsh black
- High contrast where it matters (text, buttons)
- Soft, professional grays for secondary elements

## Modern Design Elements

### 1. Rounded Corners Everywhere
- **3xl radius (24px)** on cards and sections
- **Full rounded buttons** for CTAs
- **Soft edges** throughout - no harsh rectangles

### 2. Modern Card Design
- Clean white cards with subtle borders
- Generous padding and breathing room
- Hover effects with scale and shadow
- Number badges with rounded backgrounds

### 3. Spacing & Layout
- **32px (py-32)** section spacing
- Generous white space between elements
- Modern grid layouts (3-4 columns)
- Asymmetric content blocks for interest

### 4. Modern Interactions
- **Subtle hover effects**: scale, shadow, color
- **Smooth transitions**: 300ms standard
- **Focus states**: accent color rings
- Grayscale to color on portfolio hover
